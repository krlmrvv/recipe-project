Foie Gras recipe

Foie Gras with Fig Compote description

A rich and luxurious French delicacy, foie gras is seared to perfection and paired with a sweet fig compote to balance its buttery flavor. Ideal as an appetizer or fine-dining centerpiece.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of fresh foie gras (about 1 inch thick)
  • Salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 1/2 cup dried figs (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Toasted brioche or baguette slices (for serving)

Steps

  1. Season foie gras slices with salt and pepper. Let sit at room temperature for 10 minutes.
  2. In a small saucepan, combine figs, water, balsamic vinegar, and sugar. Simmer on low heat for 10–15 minutes until figs soften and the mixture thickens into a compote.
  3. Heat a dry skillet over medium-high heat. Sear foie gras slices for 30–45 seconds per side until golden brown and crisp on the outside. Remove and place on a paper towel briefly.
  4. Plate each foie gras slice with a spoonful of fig compote and toasted brioche.
  5. Serve immediately while hot.
